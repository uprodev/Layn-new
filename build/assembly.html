What we do

We offer a range of social and professional enivroments in which to relax, meet new people and network.

LAYN Creative was born from an idea in lockdown. Our Founder and Creative Director Chris had worked as a freelancer for multiple creative agencies as well as acting as lead designer for multiple brands for over a decade. Through lockdown he formed a unique way to utilise working remotely with his network of likeminded creatives to replace the on site in house design teams.

At LAYN Creative we have continued to develop this structure to create bespoke teams from our collection of approved creative freelancers to suit our clients briefs. Our core team consists of lead creatives in each of their fields who, along with our expereinced project managers bring together the perfect teams.
